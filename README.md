# 📊 Telecom Customer Retention & Revenue Performance Dashboard

> **Business Intelligence Dashboard for Customer Retention Decision Support using Microsoft Excel**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an Executive Management Dashboard developed in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze customer churn, customer retention, and revenue performance for a telecom company.

The objective of this project is to transform raw customer data into meaningful business insights that help management identify high-risk customer segments, understand churn drivers, and make informed strategic decisions to improve customer retention.

The dashboard follows a complete Business Intelligence workflow, including:

- Data Cleaning
- Data Preparation
- KPI Development
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Business Analysis
- Executive Dashboard Design
- Business Storytelling
- Strategic Recommendations

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Telecom companies lose customers due to various factors such as contract type, pricing, service quality, and customer support.

Management needs an interactive dashboard to answer critical business questions like:

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- Which services have the highest churn?
- Which customer segments should be prioritized?
- How can customer retention be improved?
- Which factors have the biggest business impact?

# 📈 Dashboard Analysis

The dashboard includes analysis across multiple business dimensions:

- Customer Churn by Contract Type
- Customer Churn by Customer Tenure
- Customer Churn by Internet Service
- Impact of Technical Support on Customer Churn
- Customer Churn by Payment Method
- Customer Churn by Monthly Charge Band

Interactive slicers allow users to filter the dashboard by:

- Contract Type
- Internet Service
- Payment Method
- Senior Citizen

---

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Overall customer churn rate is **26.54%**
- Month-to-Month contracts have the highest churn rate (**42.71%**)
- Customers within their first year have the highest churn (**47.44%**)
- Fiber Optic customers exhibit significantly higher churn than DSL customers
- Customers without Technical Support are nearly **3× more likely to churn**
- Electronic Check users have the highest churn rate (**45.29%**)
- Customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to churn

---

# 💡 Strategic Recommendations

- Increase migration to annual and long-term contracts
- Bundle Technical Support with premium plans
- Strengthen customer onboarding during the first year
- Promote AutoPay adoption through customer incentives
- Review pricing strategy for high monthly charge customers
- Prioritize retention campaigns for Fiber Optic customers
